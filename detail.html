Welcome to Colonial Networks: Provenance Stories

Quick tips to explore the map, timeline, and networks:

Map

  • Click a circle marker to see provenance events for that place.
  • Markers with a small badge show how many events are linked to that location.
  • Dashed red lines show movement from one place to another for an event.
  • Gray route lines indicate related connections. You can click a route to open its event.

Provenance panel

  • Use the left and right arrows to move between events.
  • Click the event thumbnail to open a larger image with source details.
  • Magnify network: use the “enlarge” button to open an interactive view of the event’s network.

Timeline

  • The horizontal timeline lists all events in order.
  • Click any timeline card to open that event.
  • Prev / Next buttons scroll the timeline.

Provenance text

  • Clickable provenance sentences will jump to the matching story.
  • Related items in the text and timeline highlight together so you can track context.

Social network view

  • Open the Show Social Network view to see people and relationships.
  • Zoom and pan with your mouse or trackpad; drag nodes to adjust layout.
  • Hover a node to focus its connections. Use Reset Zoom to return to the default view.

You can reopen these instructions anytime using the small icon in the bottom right.
